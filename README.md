# Atlantis University Landing Page

Intended URL path: `/for/atlantis-university`

## Purpose

A one-page, branded pitch written for the Atlantis University MSAI program team and for whoever runs international admissions. Jake hands a prospect his phone at the booth and the page renders as though it came out of the Atlantis marketing shop.

## Content shape

- Hero headline is the section 7 Headline verbatim from `fit-analysis.md`.
- Meta description is the opening hook from section 5 of `fit-analysis.md`.
- Body pitch is adapted from `fit-analysis.md` section 7 (Opening, Frame, Case, Paper, Credit, CTA).
- CTA routes to Matt Creamer's Calendly link.

## Stack

Single static HTML page plus one CSS file. Open Sans from Google Fonts matches the atlantisuniversity.edu stack. No JavaScript. Mobile-responsive at 375px.

## Files

- `index.html`
- `styles.css`
- `logo.png` (copied from `Brand Pack/assets/logo.png`)
- `README.md`

## Brand tokens

Pulled directly from `Brand Pack/brand-pack.json`. Dominant color is Atlantis red `#EA1C24` on white, with Open Sans throughout, a red-accented CTA button, and photography-free composition that mirrors the Atlantis homepage density.

## Disclosure compliance

- No pricing or revenue terms appear anywhere on the page (value and scope language only).
- Every URL is a real `<a href target="_blank" rel="noopener">` element.
- KPMG is framed as "KPMG UK (Big Four)".
- NLP Logix mention is followed by "in machine learning since 2011" and "over 150 data scientists".
- Eduba appears only in the footer line ("Prepared by Eduba, eduba.io") and as the named case study source. No Eduba logo, no Eduba colors at the top.
- Prospect's voice throughout (second-person, warm, student-first framing applied to faculty).
- Training stat uses canonical "1,500+ people" figure.
